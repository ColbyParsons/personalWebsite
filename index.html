About

I'm a student of the Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. My interests in computer science span data visualization, algorithms, HCI, concurrency and video games. Additionally I'm an avid learner and I love tackling new and unfamiliar computer science challenges. Currently I work as a full stack developer at Tangam Gaming Systems and I am conducting HCI research at the University of Waterloo.

Outside computer science I live a healthy active life. I currently work as a Residence Life Don at the University of Waterloo. During my past 2 school terms I held the position of VP Internal of the Biology Undergraduate Society. Some of my favourite pastimes include cooking, cycling and playing chess.
